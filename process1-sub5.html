Process 1.5: Delivery & Receipt

Purpose: To initiate a procurement request within the department.
Steps:

  1. User logs in to the procurement system.
  2. Fills out the request form with required details.
  3. Attaches necessary supporting documents.
  4. Submits the request for review.
Responsible: All staff requiring procurement.
Related Documents: Procurement Request Form (PRF-001).
